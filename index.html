The Cock
& the Jewel

A Cock, scratching for food
for himself and his hens,
found a precious stone;
on which he said:
"If thy owner had found thee, and not I,
he would have taken thee up,
and have set thee in thy first estate;
but I have found thee for no purpose.
I would rather have one barleycorn
than all the jewels in the world."

The Lion in love

A Lion once fell in love with a beautiful maiden
and proposed marriage to her parents.

The old people did not know what to say.
They did not like to give their daughter to the Lion,
yet they did not wish to enrage the King of Beasts.

At last the father said:
"We feel highly honoured by your Majesty's proposal,
but you see our daughter is a tender young thing,
and we fear that in the vehemence of your affection
you might possibly do her some injury

Might I venture to suggest that your Majesty
should have your claws removed,
and your teeth extracted, then we would
gladly consider your proposal again."

The Lion was so much in love that he had
his claws trimmed and his big teeth taken out.
But when he came again to the parents of the young girl
they simply laughed in his face,
and bade him do his worst.
